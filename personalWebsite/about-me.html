Who am I?

I am a gamer who likes to program. I play games, examine parts that could have been done better, and think of ways that I could improve the code. I am also a critic, who can explain to people why what they did was wrong in great detail, and tell them how they should alter it to fix it. I love games with stories or in depth characters, something to latch onto and develop, my love of stories includes a wide variety of books where I can further develop my creativity.
